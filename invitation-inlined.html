Dear Friends and Family,

Please join us on September 1st, 2018 as we celebrate our marriage at The Harbor Room, 8 Norwood Court in Gloucester, Massachusetts.

A ceremony will be held on the upper level of The Harbor Room at 6:00pm. It will consist of readings and music by friends and family. All guests are welcome to attend.

The reception will take place at 7:00pm on The Harbor Room dock. No need to follow a dress code. Heels and Havaianas are equally welcome! Depending on the weather, you may wish to bring a sweater or light coat for later in the evening.

A variety of Brazilian dishes will be served buffet-style. There will be options for veggie-lovers and meat-lovers alike. The Schooner Festival fireworks begin at 9:15pm and should be visible from the venue.

Because our wedding coincides with the Schooner Festival weekend, we encourage out-of-towners to book your accommodations well ahead of time. Visit the travel page of our website for more info.
